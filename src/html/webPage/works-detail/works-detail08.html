国际通道 International Passageway

装置 Installation

顔磊与傅洁合作完成的作品，艺术家利用以前曾经居住过的房子的各种数据制做了一个通道。那段时期艺术家正频繁往返于国际参加各种艺术活动，有机会出入世界各地的机场，这个通道的样子就是按照乘客登机时从检票口到机舱之间，由摆渡车连接的那段距离。（即一个不具有任何属性的中间地带），其时中国的当代艺术正处在一个急速国际化的特殊时期.

This work is a collaboration between Yan Lei and Fu Jie. The artist used the measurements of all of his previous homes in order to construct a "passageway". In this period, artist is often involved in numerous international art events and he frequently have to pass through airports in order to travel around the world. The passageway alludes to the spaces between passing the ticket through the boarding gate and the airplane cabin – or the area travelled when sitting on the express shuttle (a non-space that belongs to nowhere). It’s a work that mirrors the unprecedented era of fast-paced globalization of contemporary Chinese art.
