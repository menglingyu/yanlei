追光 Sparkling

画面来自于艺术家自己脑子里的一些影像记忆片断，这些图片带着闪烁的光芒，作为一种带着炫耀方式进行着叙事。

The paintings are composed of fragments of images and memories taken from the mind of the artist. These pictures carry with them flashes of light in order to direct the way for an unfolding narrative.
