永恒价值系列 Eternal Value

作品由锆石、霓虹灯、金属等材质打造而成，充满了昂贵的奢侈品气息。这也是在艺术家与施华洛世奇合作的作品。十字架造型以及其上的sex、show、speed、suck等四个词汇亦让此作品与当今流行的文化和观念产生了关联。这个作品刺激了名利场的心理学

For this work he used different materials from zircon, to neon, metal and other objects, to make the piece as if it was full of an “expensive”, “luxurious” atmosphere. This piece has been realized in collaboration with Swarovski. On the artwork, whose shape resembles that of a cross, he inserted four words: sex, show, speed and suck. The usage of these words is to stress the relation of the artwork with todays popular culture and ideas, stimulating also the psychology of a “Vanity Fair”.
